The analysis will use ZIPCODE as proxy to neighborhoods. The unit of analysis will be over 90 zipcodes in Chicago. The study will collect data on:
•	Income for each zipcode, collecting from US Census Bureau 
•	Foursquare location data on parks, restaurants, grocery stores, or other businesses that are measuring the infrastructure and quality of live within a neighborhood.

Methodology (brief)
•	Compile zip code list, geocode the neighborhoods, and add income data to the list.
•	Identify the clusters of neighborhoods based on the location and income. Map the clusters of neighborhoods.
•	Compare the businesses and infrastructure across neighborhood cluster.
